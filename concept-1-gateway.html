The Protocol

Credit infrastructure
built for the open web.

Lacus routes and orchestrates debt capital without ever taking custody of funds. Every position is on-chain, every tranche is inspectable.

🔗

On-Chain Issuance

Companies tokenize debt instruments directly on Solana. KYC-gated, covenant-defined, instantly verifiable by any investor.

Transparent Portfolios

Every credit position is inspectable. No black boxes. Structured tranches, loan agreements, and financials live on-chain — not in a PDF.
